# Rice_Yield_CMIP5_CMIP6
Processed datasets and Python scripts supporting the analysis of rice yield changes under CMIP5 and CMIP6 climate scenarios in China.
This repository contains the processed, analysis-ready datasets used in this study. The datasets support the main rice yield and climate analyses presented in the associated manuscript.

The repository includes the following files:

1. CMIP5_yearly_yield.csv
   Annual simulated rice yields under CMIP5 climate scenarios.

2. CMIP6_yearly_yield.csv
   Annual simulated rice yields under CMIP6 climate scenarios.

3. model_yield_change_irrigated.csv
   Summary statistics of simulated rice yield changes under irrigated conditions for individual climate models and scenarios.

4. model_yield_change_rainfed.csv
   Summary statistics of simulated rice yield changes under rainfed conditions for individual climate models and scenarios.

5. station_climate_anomaly_CMIP5.csv
   Annual mean and inter-model standard deviation of six climate variables (minimum temperature, maximum temperature, solar radiation, relative humidity, precipitation, and wind speed) under CMIP5 climate scenarios.

6. station_climate_anomaly_CMIP6.csv
   Annual mean and inter-model standard deviation of six climate variables (minimum temperature, maximum temperature, solar radiation, relative humidity, precipitation, and wind speed) under CMIP6 climate scenarios.
